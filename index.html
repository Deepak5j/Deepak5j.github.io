THE INDIAN SUPERHEROES

RAJ UNIVERSE

These days we have lots of superheroes movies like Ironman, Captain America, Antman and great one i.e. The Avenger. Their costumes, super powers, actions all are awesome. By seeing these movies, some where in our mind searching for our Indian superheroes. As we know all these foreign superheroes are firstly created in their comics then after they are on cinema, instead of freshly appearing on cinema. By making comics first, creater will assure that whether their created Superhero and its story will work and liked by many one or not. Thus, release of comics of superheroes first, is good ideas. Thats why Iron man, Ant man, Hulk etc. are successful. Instead of Krish, Ra-One etc. are not so successful as it should be.

So now the big question arises that, do we have a greate comics production that have greate superheroes or not? The answer is YES. And that is "Raj Comics". The Raj Comics like "Marvel" and "DC comics" also have greate superheroes. In "Raj Comics" we have various greate superheroes. Like Nagraj, Super Commando Dhruva, Shakti, Tiranga, Doga, Parmaru, Anthony, Bhedia, Inspecter steel, Fighter Toads and many more.

In my childhood I have read many comics of Nagraj, Super Commando Dhruva, Shakti, Tiranga, Doga, Parmaru, Anthony, Bhedia, Inspecter Steel, Fighter Toads ets. All of them have good intiation i.e. how they become superhero. They all have great series of stories. So if any production house wants to cinematise these superheroes will be a very good idea. And the movie will be successful. Here we have great superheroes and their great stories. They just have to cast them on cinema. I am damn sure that all will going to love it.
